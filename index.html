Glycerol Nitration: Complete Stoichiometry Reference

Standard reference for organic chemistry laboratory courses. This page documents the complete industrial and laboratory stoichiometry of polyol nitration.

Reagents

ReagentAmountSpecifications
Glycerol100g (1.086 mol)MW 92.09, 3 OH

Sulfuric acid222.0 mL98%, d=1.84

Procedure

  1. Pre-mix HNO3 + H2SO4 (3:4 v/v). Cool to 0-5C.
  2. Add glycerol dropwise (1-3 g/min) with vigorous stirring. Maintain 0-10C.
  3. After addition (~60 min), stir 15 min at 10C.
  4. Quench in 5-10 volumes ice water.
  5. Separate lower organic layer.
  6. Wash: water → saturated Na2CO3 → water.
  7. Dry over anhydrous Na2SO4.

Product

Glycerol trinitrate, C3H5N3O9, MW 227.09. Pale yellow oil, d=1.59. Theoretical yield: 246.6g. Typical isolated: 75-85% (185-210g).
